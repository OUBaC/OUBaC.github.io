Clubnight

  • Clubnights are recreational sessions for players of all abilities!
  • Clubnights are open to everyone, both university members and members of the public.
  • Clubnight sessions are supervised by a member of the university badminton club, who ensure that everyone gets an equal number of games.
  • When playing you should be wearing non-marking trainers or court shoes.
  • Rackets and plastic shuttles are provided at the session! It is recommended to bring your own racket if you have one.
  • Most clubnight sessions are run at Iffley Road Sports Centre, Iffley Rd, Oxford OX4 1EQ. Some sessions may also be run at the nearby Magdalen College School, or Oxford High School in Summertown.
  • Session times are updated every week on PlayWaze, booking for each session opens three days before the session start time.
    • E.g. a session on Friday at 18:00 will open for booking on Tuesday at 18:00.
  • Memberships allow you to book onto sessions for free after purchasing, see below for prices and dates for memberships.
