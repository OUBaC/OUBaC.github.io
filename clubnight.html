Clubnight

  • Clubnight is for players of all abilities!
  • Clubnight is for having fun and is a good opportunity to make friends outside college.
  • A pegboard system is usually operated to ensure everyone gets an equal number of games.
  • You should be wearing non-marking trainers or court shoes when playing.
  • The club provides plenty of plastic shuttles.
  • To attend any Clubnight sessions this year you will need to 'Join the Club' on Playwaze.

Membership

We offer a number of membership options for those wishing to attend Clubnight, which offers flexibility to our members:

  • Termly: £30
  • Yearly (Terms only): £70
  • Yearly (Terms + Vacs): £85
  • Pay per short vac: £15
  • Pay per long vac: £30
  • Or £5 per session without membership

Clubnight Tournament

Alongside regular recreational sessions, all OUBaC members will have the opportunity to play in termly tournaments, with an emphasis on fun and socialising rather than competition. The tournaments will either run using a team format or be appropriately handicapped, to ensure that all entrants have a chance of securing a confectionary based prize.
