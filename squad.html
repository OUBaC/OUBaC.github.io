Squad

  • The squad consists of 5 competitive teams (3 men’s team and 2 women’s team) and is selected by the team captains from the trials in Michaelmas term. Come along to tryout!
  • All our Squad players have the opportunity to get trained by top-level coaches multiple times a week.
  • Our teams compete in the BUCS leagues:
    • M1 – Men’s Premier South
    • M2 – Men’s Midlands 2B
    • W1 – Women’s Midlands 1A
  • We also have teams in the Oxfordshire Badminton League:
    • M3 – Men's Division 2
    • W2 – Ladies Division 1
    • Mixed – Mixed Division 1
  • Both men’s and women’s badminton have Oxford University Discretionary Full Blue status! For more information click here.

Development Squad

  • Development squad members don’t compete in a league but get the opportunity to train with and learn from the main squad.
