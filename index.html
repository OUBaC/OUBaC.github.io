Clubnight

Clubnight sessions are friendly sessions open to everyone! A player of any standard will be any able to get good games here. Clubnight costs £40 for a year, £15 for a term or £5 for a session and it can be paid on the day.

S

Squad

The Squad offers the best players the opportunity to test their abilities in a competitive setting. Members of the squad compete in the local leagues and in B.U.C.S.
