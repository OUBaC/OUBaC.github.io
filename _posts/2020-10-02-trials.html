Getting to Trials

To get to St Gregory the Great Catholic School it is a 10 minute cycle or 40 minute walk from the centre of town. The nearest bus stop is Shelley Road which is stopped at by the: 1, 5, 10, 11, 12 and U5BROOKESbus buses.
