Sign up for the Oxford University Badminton Squad Trials! OUBaC has three men's teams and three women's teams, competing in BUCS (Inter-Uni), local league, and in Varsity against Cambridge.

All trials will be held at Iffley Sports Centre (Iffley Rd, Oxford OX4 1EQ). They will either be in Acer or Main; if you don't know where these are, you can ask at the reception.

Women's trials will be held on Saturday 12th October 15:30-17:30 (Main)

Men's trials will be held on Saturday 12th October 17:30-19:30 (Acer), with another session on Sunday 13th October 18:00-19:30 (Main).
