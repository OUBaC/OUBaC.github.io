Trials 2023

Sign up for the Oxford University Badminton Squad Trials! OUBaC has four men's teams and three women's teams, competing in BUCS (Inter-Uni), local league, and in Varsity against Cambridge.

All trials will be held at Iffley Sports Centre (Iffley Rd, Oxford OX4 1EQ). Men's trials will be held on Saturday 7th October, sign up to either the 13:15-15:15 or 15:15-17:15 sessions. Women's trials will be held Saturday 7th October 17:30-19:30.

Watch your emails as we'll be sending out trial allocations and confirmations by Friday evening!
